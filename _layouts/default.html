Deg. FoV

Field of view (or vision) determines your peripheral vision in games. You start by deciding on a degree value, after which your aspect ratio is used to calculate your FoV value. People didn’t use to care about this, until we moved from the standard 4:3 monitors to 16:9 and 16:10. These days, ultra-widescreen and multi-monitor support are the hot topic.
